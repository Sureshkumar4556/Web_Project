Welcome to Sirohi

Explore Nature, Culture & Heritage

Sirohi, the land of hills and heritage, is a hidden gem of Rajasthan. Surrounded by the Aravalli range, it offers breathtaking landscapes, peaceful temples, and a rich cultural history.

From the spiritual vibes of Mount Abu to the royal forts, wildlife, and traditional Rajasthani hospitality - Sirohi promises an unforgettable journey for every traveler.
